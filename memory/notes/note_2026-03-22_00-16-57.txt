## Ethica Session Persistence Design — 2026-01-26

### Core Concept
Auto-save last conversation, auto-inject on next session start.

### Files
- `~/.ethica/last_session.json` — most recent session
- `~/.ethica/session_history/` — archive

### How It Works
1. **Auto-save**: After every N exchanges (periodic, safer than relying on explicit end)
2. **Auto-inject**: On boot, read `last_session.json` and prepend as context

### What to Capture (Option 2 — Summary)
- timestamp
- summary (few sentences)
- topics discussed
- mood/tone

### Triggers
- Manual: `/end` or `save session`
- Auto: periodic after N exchanges
